## 1. What Are Cookies and Similar Technologies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website.  
They allow a website to recognize your device, remember certain technical information, and function reliably.

In addition to cookies, websites may use similar technologies, such as:

— local storage,  
— session storage,  
— technical pixels,  
— server-side logs.

For clarity, this Notice refers to all such technologies collectively as “cookies”.

## 2. Why We Use Cookies

The Website uses cookies solely for limited and legitimate purposes, consistent with the philosophical and non-commercial nature of the project.

Cookies may be used to:

— ensure the basic technical operation of the Website;  
— maintain security and integrity (e.g., preventing abuse or attacks);  
— support error detection and diagnostics;  
— understand aggregated usage patterns to improve stability and performance, without identifying individual users.

We do not use cookies to:

— track users across different websites;  
— create detailed personal profiles;  
— deliver behavioral or targeted advertising;  
— sell or share personal data.
